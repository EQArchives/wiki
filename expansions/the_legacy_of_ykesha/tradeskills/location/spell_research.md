# Spell Research Trade Supplies

**Essential vendors** for **Parchment**, **Quill**, class-specific research tomes (e.g., **Lexicon** for Wizards, **Tome of Endless Enchantments** for Enchanters), **Elemental Grimoire** (Mages), **Book of Dark Bindings** (Necromancers), and **LoY Advanced Research Books** (e.g., Yaeth's Compendium, Toharon's Memoirs).

> **ℹ️ Tip:** Use `/find spell research merchant` or `/find <item name>` for quick location. Many cities have generic "spell research merchants" stocking basics (parchment, quill, inks). **LoY books** are **Legacy of Ykesha-specific** for level 50+ swatches.
