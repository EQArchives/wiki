## Progression Summary

**Shadows of Luclin Path:**
1. Level 51 → General Abilities (stats, resistances, utilities)
2. Level 55 → Archetype Abilities (Priest or Melee foundation)
3. Level 59 → Beastlord Class Abilities (class-specific powers)
4. Level 61 → Planar Advancement (enhanced stat/resistance trees)
5. Level 62-63 → Hybrid Planar Abilities (cross-archetype synergies)

**Gates of Discord Expansion:**
- Level 63+ → Beastlord GoD Abilities (pet mastery, advanced combat, spell refinement)
