# Shadows of Luclin - Visible Armor Sets

## Overview

This wiki documents the visible armor sets available in the EverQuest expansion "The Shadows of Luclin." This page is divided into two sections: **Verified Armor Sets** (with complete quest and acquisition data) and **Sets Requiring Verification** (armor sets that have been reported but lack confirmed drop locations and detailed acquisition information).

---

## Verified Quest Armor Sets

### How Quest Armor Works

Each class has a unique quest-based visible armor set available in The Shadows of Luclin. These armor quests require the collection of specific components that drop from named mobs and regular mobs throughout Luclin zones. Each piece requires:

1. A **Jewel** (from specific mobs)
2. A **Mark** (from specific mobs)
3. One or more **Miscellaneous Items** (from specific mobs)

The pieces are then handed to NPCs in specific zones to receive the completed armor piece.
